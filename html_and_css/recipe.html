McCall's Baked Macaroni and Cheese

Introduction

Total Time: 35 minutes

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

This is one of my favorite recipes from McCall's Cook Book. I have been eating it since I was a kid, when my mother made it for me. Now that I'm an adult, I make it for myself. Enjoy!

Ingredients

Servings: 4

  • 1 (8 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
  • 2 cups skim milk
  • 2 cups grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 large tomato, thickly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley

Cooking Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees f.
  2. Cook macaroni as package directs, drain.
  3. While cooking macaroni, melt butter in medium saucepan.
  4. Remove from heat and whisk in flour, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  5. Gradually stir in milk.
  6. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat and simmer 1-2 minutes.
  7. Stir in 1 1/2 cup cheese and the cooked macaroni.
  8. Spray a 1 1/2 quart shallow casserole dish with cooking spray. Pour in macaroni and cheese.
  9. Arrange tomato slices over top. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top. Sprinkle parsley on cheese.
  10. Bake about 15 minutes uncovered until cheese is golden brown.
